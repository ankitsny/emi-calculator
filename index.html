↳ Part Payments / Prepayments

▾

One-time lump-sum payments applied before that month's EMI. Choose to apply to Standard only, OD only, or both. Free prepayment on floating loans — most banks allow this.

↳ OD Balance — Period Overrides

▾

Override the default OD balance for a specific month range. Model bonus months, salary hikes, lean periods. Ranges can overlap — last matching range wins. Any calendar month that falls between two override ranges (a gap) uses ₹0 parked OD, not the default. Till End appears only on the last period row. Click Edit on a row to show Apply and Cancel; Apply saves that row and runs Calculate & Compare. When you Calculate, if a new period starts after an open-ended one, the previous period is closed automatically the month before that start.
